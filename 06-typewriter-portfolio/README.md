## 🧭 About

B.Tech Computer Engineering, University of Mumbai. Now AI Systems Architect Intern at Deloitte, where I design the retrieval, memory, and agent layers that hold LLM apps together. On my own time I ship libraries — small, opinionated primitives that solve a silent-failure mode I got tired of watching people ship past.
